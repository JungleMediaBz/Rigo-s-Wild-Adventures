About Rigo's Wild Adventures

Welcome to Rigo's Wild Adventures, your gateway to an unforgettable journey through the heart of Belize's natural beauty and rich cultural heritage. Founded by Rigo, a passionate local guide, our company is dedicated to providing authentic, sustainable, and memorable adventures. Born in Belize City to a family from Guatemala, Rigo was raised on the beautiful Arnold Farms, a banana farm where he spent 18 years of his life. We believe that travel should be transformative, and we're here to help you create memories that will last a lifetime while supporting the local communities that make Belize so special.

Discover Belize

Belize is a small country located on the eastern coast of Central America, bordered by Mexico to the north and Guatemala to the west and south. Despite its small size, Belize offers an incredible diversity of landscapes, from pristine Caribbean beaches to dense tropical rainforests.

The country is renowned for its barrier reef, the second-largest in the world, making it a paradise for snorkeling and diving enthusiasts. Inland, you'll find ancient Mayan ruins, extensive cave systems, and lush jungles teeming with wildlife.

Belize's rich cultural heritage is reflected in its diverse population, which includes descendants of the ancient Maya, Creole, Garifuna, Mestizo, and other ethnic groups. This cultural diversity is celebrated through vibrant festivals, delicious cuisine, and warm hospitality that welcomes visitors from around the world.
